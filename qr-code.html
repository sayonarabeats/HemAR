Hema AR Navigation

Scan this QR code with your smartphone to experience the interactive AR store navigation

QR code will be generated when deployed

How to use:

  1. Scan the QR code with your smartphone camera
  2. Open the link in your web browser
  3. Allow camera permissions when prompted
  4. Choose your preferred language (English or Dutch)
  5. Point your camera at a flat surface and tap "Place Store"
  6. Select a department to see navigation arrows
  7. Follow the arrows to reach your destination

Note: This AR experience works best on modern smartphones with AR capabilities in a well-lit environment.
